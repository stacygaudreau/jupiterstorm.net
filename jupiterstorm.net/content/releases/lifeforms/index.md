Human beings have always asked questions about the stars. It's as natural as breathing.

Lifeforms is a two track EP which explores the miracle of life on planet Earth, through the lens of our relationship with the vast cosmos of outer space.

Featuring hypnotic rhythms, dreamy melodies and lush, ethereal trance energy, the single is ready to leave a lasting impression on the dancefloor.

Flowki rounds out the EP with an absolutely stunning remix of the original track! Known for his beautiful, dynamic arrangements and uplifting vibes, his masterful reimagination of "Lifeforms" is right at home with the stars.
