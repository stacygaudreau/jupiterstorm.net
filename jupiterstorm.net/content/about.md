Jupiter Storm's melody-driven and cinematic style is bringing a fresh sound to the psytrance genre. His music blends the emotional storytelling of progressive trance with the hypnotic energy of psytrance, leaning on a world of detailed, immersive atmospheres which draw listeners in.

Behind the project is Montreal's Stacy Gaudreau, who has a technical background developing electronic circuits for analog modular synthesizers, and later, software audio effect plugins. This foundation in software, audio science and engineering quietly informs the detail supporting his releases.
